Why 12:12 exists

Founded to restore more than curb appeal.

12:12 Property Services was founded to create opportunity for men in sober living at RAF Recovery. Inspired by Romans 12:12 and the 12 steps, the team serves with integrity, faith, and purpose.

Creating opportunity, serving with purpose, and transforming properties and lives.

Recovery partnership

Connected to Recovered and Free

12:12 works closely with RAF, a Hubbard-based men's recovery program. Visitors who want to support the mission can learn more and ask about scholarship support for men seeking recovery.

Services

Practical help for properties that need a reset.

From regular maintenance to one-time recovery jobs, the service mix is built for outdoor spaces that need attention, cleanup, and a fresh start.

01

Lawn Care & Landscaping

Mowing, edging, trimming, basic planting support, and property cleanup that helps a place look cared for again.

02

Pet Waste Cleanup

Scheduled or one-time yard cleanup for busy households, rental properties, and outdoor spaces that need a sanitary reset.

03

Power Washing

Driveways, sidewalks, patios, siding touchups, and exterior surfaces refreshed with careful pressure and clear expectations.

04

Leaf & Debris Cleanup

Seasonal leaves, branches, light storm debris, and neglected edges cleared so the property feels usable again.

05

Property Recovery

Cleanouts, overgrowth control, curb appeal projects, and step-by-step recovery plans for homes that have fallen behind.

06

Custom Outdoor Help

Odd jobs, prep work, haul-off coordination, and honest referrals when a project needs a licensed trade.

Approach

A clear process for bringing order back.

Recovery work is practical work: show up, assess what is there, take the next right step, and leave the place better than it was found.

01

Walk the Property

Review priorities, access points, safety concerns, and what success should look like.

02

Set the Plan

Choose the right service path for the yard, surface, season, budget, and timeline.

03

Do the Work

Handle the visible cleanup and the small details that make the property feel respected.

04

Keep It Moving

Recommend maintenance, follow-up visits, or the next restoration step when needed.
